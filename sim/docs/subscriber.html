Making Calls

To make a call, lift the receiver and crank the magneto. When the operator responds, tell them who you wish to contact. The operator will connect you.

Ending Calls

When finished with your call, hang up and crank the magneto. This will inform the operator that your are finished and the circuit may be released to use for another call ("Ring Off").

Receiving Calls

When the telephone rings, lift the receiver and respond. Either the operator or the calling party will reply.
